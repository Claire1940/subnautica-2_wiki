The Architect Organs are found within the **Crystal Caves Cache**. This area is known for its stunning, yet perilous, environment and requires advanced diving capabilities.

<Callout type="warning" title="Depth Warning">
The Crystal Caves Cache is located at depths exceeding 800 meters. Ensure your Seatruck is equipped with a **Depth Module Mark 3** to withstand the immense pressure and avoid hull damage. Without it, your vehicle will be crushed.
</Callout>

### Navigation to the Crystal Caves Cache

Follow these general directions to reach the Architect Organ location:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start at Delta Station Dock">
    Begin your journey at the Delta Station Dock. From here, you will need to head approximately 600 meters in an easterly direction. Keep a close eye on your compass.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter Crystal Caves">
    The entrance to the Crystal Caves is marked by large purple crystals. You'll need to descend into a large hole to access the main cave system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate Deep Sections">
    Once inside the Crystal Caves, proceed in a generally northern direction, making your way deeper. This area is a maze of twists and turns, often requiring careful maneuvering of your Seatruck. Stay to the left side as much as possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate the Cache Entrance">
    You will eventually reach a specific area teeming with crystals and potentially hostile fauna like **Ghost Leviathans**. Look for a distinct entrance that leads further down, marked by glowing green structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Retrieve the Organs">
    Exit your Seatruck once you reach approximately 824 meters deep. Follow the pathway on foot until you find an open container holding the **Architect Organs**. Scan them to obtain the blueprint.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
Before venturing into the Crystal Caves, ensure your Seatruck is fully repaired and consider bringing extra power cells. The journey is long and can be quite dangerous.
</Callout>

### Comparison of Architect Body Components

The Architect Body requires three distinct components for its construction: the Organs, the Skeleton, and the Tissues. Each is found in a unique cache across Planet 4546B.

| Component      | Location             | Description                                     | Key Feature                                     |
| :------------- | :------------------- | :---------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------- |
| **Organs**     | Crystal Caves Cache  | Biocomposite & cybernetic network               | Central energy & neurological hub               |
| **Skeleton**   | Deep Lilypads Cave Cache | Dense synthetic skeletal structure              | Extreme hardness (Mohs 10), impact resistant    |
| **Tissues**    | Arctic Spires Cache  | Leather-like alien tissue                       | High impact resistance, low elasticity          |

Once you have scanned the Architect Organs and obtained their blueprint, the next step is to gather the necessary materials and fabricate the component. This is done at the Fabricator Base.

### Materials Required for Architect Organs

The crafting recipe for Architect Organs has been updated since early access. Here are the materials you'll need for the final release version:

| Material         | Quantity | Source                                     |
| :--------------- | :------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| **Ion Cube**     | 1        | Deep Twisty Bridges, Crystal Caves         |
| **Ribbon Plants**| 2        | Deep Twisty Bridges, Lilypads Crevice      |
| **Kyanite**      | 3        | Crystal Caves, Deep Lilypads Cave          |

<Callout type="success" title="Preparation Tip">
Gathering these materials can be time-consuming. Prioritize exploring the Deep Twisty Bridges and Crystal Caves for Ion Cubes and Kyanite, and the Lilypads Crevice for Ribbon Plants before attempting to craft.
</Callout>

### Assembling Al-An's New Vessel

After successfully crafting the Architect Organs, you must also craft the Skeleton and Tissues. Once all three components are ready, you can assemble Al-An's complete vessel at the Fabricator Base.
