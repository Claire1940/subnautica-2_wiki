<Section icon={Target} title="Efficient Nickel Ore Farming Methods">
Finding **Nickel Ore** can be tricky due to its color blending with the environment. Employing specific tools and strategies can significantly improve your yield. The goal is to collect the approximately seven pieces of **Nickel Ore** needed for all essential upgrades as quickly and safely as possible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Gear">
    Equip your **Seatruck** with at least a Depth Module Mark 1. Consider bringing a **Mineral Detector** if you have one, as it greatly aids in locating ore. Ensure you have enough oxygen tanks or a Rebreather for extended dives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Deep Lilypads Cave">
    Head to the **Deep Lilypads Cave** biome. A good starting point is often north of your drop pod, looking for a distinct mountain formation. Place a beacon for easy navigation back to your starting point. Aim for a depth of around 220-300 meters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Search the Cave Walls">
    Once inside the cave system, specifically in the Deep Lilypads Cave, hug the ocean floor and look for a channel or structural formation. **Nickel Ore** often spawns on the walls of these channels. Turning off your vehicle's headlights can sometimes make the ore stand out more against the ambient light.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize the Mineral Detector">
    If you have a **Mineral Detector**, activate it frequently. While it can sometimes point to inaccessible deposits, it's invaluable for revealing hidden **Nickel Ore** outcrops. Mineral Detectors can be found in Emergency Supply Caches, caves near Delta Station, and Twisty Bridges tech sites.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Leverage Sea Monkeys">
    Once you have Al-An in your head, **Sea Monkeys** become friendly and will bring you resources. To specifically get **Nickel Ore**, pin a crafting recipe that requires it (e.g., Seatruck Depth Module Mark 3). The **Sea Monkeys** will then prioritize bringing you components for that pinned recipe, including **Nickel Ore**. Craft other required items beforehand to increase the chance of them bringing nickel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Visibility Enhancement">
When searching in darker areas like the Deep Lilypads Cave, try turning off your vehicle's headlights. The subtle glow of the **Nickel Ore** can sometimes be more apparent in the ambient light, preventing it from blending too much with the illuminated terrain.
</Callout>

</Section>
