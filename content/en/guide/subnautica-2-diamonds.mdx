<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Diamond Farming Strategy">
Efficiently collecting diamonds requires a combination of good navigation and the right tools. Follow these steps to maximize your haul from the Delta Island region.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation and Equipment">
    Before heading out, ensure you have a **Grav Trap** (or two for maximum efficiency) and your **Sea Truck** or Sea Glide. The Sea Truck offers more storage and protection, making it the preferred choice for longer expeditions. Make sure your oxygen tank is upgraded for extended dives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Delta Island">
    Start your journey from the Delta Station Dock. Head towards Delta Island. Once you're near the island, position yourself so the island is on your left side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Follow the Southwest Path">
    Proceed in a southwest direction, hugging the contours of Delta Island. You'll pass over spires and encounter signs of geothermal activity. Continue until you begin to descend into deeper waters, specifically looking for the "copper mining site."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate the Entrance">
    At the copper mining site, you'll see distinctive structures. Look for an opening on a ledge to the west. This crevice is large enough to fit your Sea Truck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Secure Parking and Deployment">
    Carefully pilot your Sea Truck into the opening. Find a safe spot away from any active lava geysers to park. Once secured, exit your Sea Truck. Deploy your **Grav Trap(s)** in the vicinity. The Grav Trap will attract nearby loose resources, including diamonds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Collect and Repeat">
    Once the Grav Trap has collected diamonds and other valuable minerals like titanium and gold, gather them. There are often multiple diamond veins in this area, sometimes up to four veins yielding 16 diamonds in total. Keep an eye out for additional diamonds you can pick up by hand, especially on higher ledges.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Grav Trap Efficiency">
Grav Traps are incredibly effective for harvesting diamonds, as they can pull multiple loose resources towards them. While they may take minor damage from geysers, the efficiency gain is well worth it.
</Callout>

</Section>
