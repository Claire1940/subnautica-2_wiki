Subnautica 2 base building is about balancing survival utility with long-term expansion. Start with a compact habitat near reliable resources, then scale into specialized wings for crafting, storage, and vehicle operations.

## Build Order for Early Progress

1. Place a starter habitat near shallow safe routes.
2. Add power first (solar in safe zones, higher-output options later).
3. Expand storage and fabrication space before decorative expansion.
4. Add docking and vehicle support when deep-biome travel begins.

## Layout Priorities

- Keep crafting, storage, and oxygen access close together.
- Reserve room for future expansion corridors.
- Separate noisy traffic areas from scanning and planning spaces.
- Design with co-op roles in mind if your group shares one base.

## Co-op Base Tips

- Split responsibilities: resource runs, power maintenance, and construction.
- Use labeled storage zones for faster team turnover.
- Keep one emergency loadout zone near exits.

## Common Mistakes

- Overbuilding decoration before stable resource flow.
- Ignoring power growth as room count increases.
- Expanding too far from key biome routes.

A strong base in Subnautica 2 should be easy to maintain, quick to navigate, and adaptable as new tools and biomes open.
