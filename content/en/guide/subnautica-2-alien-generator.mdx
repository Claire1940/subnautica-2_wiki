### Base Integration Strategies

1.  **Direct Connection**: Build your primary base segments or a power relay station directly adjacent to the generator for the most efficient power transfer.
2.  **Power Transmitters**: Use power transmitters to extend the generator's reach to distant base modules or satellite outposts. This is crucial for large-scale operations.
3.  **Prioritize High-Demand Modules**: Allocate the generator's power to energy-intensive modules first, such as advanced fabricators, water filtration systems, moonpools, and vehicle modification stations.
4.  **Backup Systems**: Even with an Alien Generator, maintaining a small backup power source (e.g., a thermal plant or bioreactor) is wise in case of unexpected events or temporary disruptions.
