A brand new mechanic introduced in the 2026 release is the DNA modification system. This allows players to physically enhance their character by harvesting genetic material from the local wildlife, including **subnautica 2 all leviathans**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Craft the Bio Sampler">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Harvest DNA">
