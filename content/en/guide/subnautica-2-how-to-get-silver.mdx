<Section icon={Swords} title="Crafting the Sonic Resonator">
The **Sonic Resonator** is an indispensable tool for efficiently harvesting large material deposits, including those containing abundant **Silver** ore. Crafting it early will significantly boost your resource gathering capabilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Basic Resources">
    Before you can even think about the Sonic Resonator, you'll need foundational resources. Focus on collecting **Copper Ore** and **Quartz**. These are typically found in abundance in the immediate vicinity of your Lifepod.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft Copper Wire and Wiring Kits">
    Once you have Copper Ore, convert it into **Copper Wire** at a Fabricator. Then, use the Copper Wire to craft **Wiring Kits**. These are intermediate components required for several advanced tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire First Silver Ore">
    This is the crucial step. You'll need at least two pieces of **Silver Ore** to craft the Sonic Resonator. Utilize the early game locations north of your Lifepod (the deep cracks) to find single Silver nodes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Craft System Chips">
    With your Wiring Kits and some additional resources (often Titanium), craft **System Chips**. These are high-level components necessary for the Sonic Resonator.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Fabricate the Sonic Resonator">
    Once you have all the necessary components (including the two Silver Ore), head to your Fabricator and craft the **Sonic Resonator**. This tool will unlock the ability to harvest large deposits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficient Harvesting">
Once you have the **Sonic Resonator**, revisit the rich deposits along the cliff's edge south of your Lifepod. These will now yield multiple pieces of **Silver** and other valuable materials, making your resource runs much more productive.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Silver Farming with Scanner Stations">
Once you've progressed a bit and gathered some initial **Silver**, the most efficient and reliable way to farm it consistently is by utilizing a **Scanner Station**. This advanced base module allows you to pinpoint resource deposits across a wide area.

### Building a Scanner Station

The Scanner Station requires specific materials, including two pieces of **Silver Ore**, which means you'll need to find some manually before you can deploy this powerful tool.

| Component      | Quantity | Source                                     | Notes                                                |
| :------------- | :------- | :----------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| **Copper Ore** | Handful  | Surface, shallow caves                     | Easily found near starting area                      |
| **Quartz**     | Handful  | Surface, shallow caves                     | Clear crystalline structures                         |
| **Silver Ore** | 2        | Single nodes (north of Lifepod), other areas | Essential for the station itself, find manually first |
| **Wiring Kit** | 1        | Fabricator (from Copper Wire)              | Basic crafting component                             |
| **System Chip**| 1        | Fabricator (from Wiring Kit, Titanium)     | Advanced crafting component                          |

<Callout type="info" title="Strategic Placement">
Consider building your first Scanner Station near your main base or in an area where you suspect rich resources might be, allowing for easy access to detected deposits.
</Callout>

### Using the Scanner Station to Find Silver

Operating the Scanner Station is straightforward and incredibly effective for locating specific resources like **Silver**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Scanner Station Console">
    Enter your Scanner Station and interact with its console. You'll see a map of the surrounding area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Silver Deposits">
    On the console interface, there will be options to scan for various materials. Select **Silver Ore** from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify Nodes on the Map">
    The Scanner Station will then display nodes representing **Silver** deposits on its holographic map. These nodes indicate the location of Silver within the station's range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mark and Navigate">
    Click on any **Silver** node you wish to find. This will place a marker on your HUD (Heads-Up Display), guiding you directly to the deposit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Harvest the Silver">
    Follow your HUD marker to the location and harvest the **Silver** ore. Remember to bring your **Sonic Resonator** if you're targeting large deposits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Expand Your Reach">
Later in the game, when you establish secondary bases in different biomes, building additional Scanner Stations will allow you to efficiently locate **Silver** and other rare materials across the entire map.
</Callout>
