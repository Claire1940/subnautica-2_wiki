<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Angel Comb">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Viral Blooms">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use the Feedback Resonator">

  <Step num="4" title="Collect Axum Vision">

After obtaining **Axum Vision**, the next critical step towards the ending of Subnautica 2 involves repairing a damaged turbine and activating alien outposts. This will restore power to crucial systems.

### Turbine Repair Process

| Step             | Action                                      | Required Materials                     | Notes                                                |
| :--------------- | :------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Scan Turbine**  | Locate the damaged turbine and scan it to get blueprints. | None                                   | Reveals required components.                         |
| **2. Craft Turbine Parts** | Fabricate the necessary components for repair. | 20 Titanium Ingots, 10 Mangalite Ingots | Requires 60 Titanium and 10 Mangalite raw materials. |
| **3. Repair Generator** | Return to the generator and use the crafted parts to repair it. | Crafted Turbine Parts                  | Power plant repairs will be completed.               |
| **4. Activate Switches** | Swim down and activate the switches to start water flow into the turbine. | None                                   | This initiates the power generation.                 |
| **5. Restart Power Plant** | Locate the main controls within the alien base to fully restart the power plant. | None                                   | Power will be restored across the alien structures.  |

<Callout type="info" title="Titanium Farming">
Large amounts of titanium can be found a few hundred meters west of the alien ruins, often in wreckages or large resource deposits. Make sure to stock up!

<Section icon={Map} title="Deciphering the Alien Language and Reaching the Ending">
With the alien structures powered up and your **Axum Vision** active, you are now ready to fully understand the alien language and uncover the game's ultimate secrets.

### Understanding the Architects

1.  **Scan the Rosetta Stone**: Return to the main alien base and locate the Rosetta Stone. Scanning it will enable you to understand the alien language, revealing glyphs that present messages in two languages, including the Architect symbols known from 4546B.
2.  **Enter the Final Alien Structure**: Proceed into the last alien structure. This area, undisturbed for ages, houses a map of the local solar system and nearby stars, including a pulsar matching known databases.
3.  **Uncover the Message**: The main message reveals a plea from the Architects: "Architects, we beg your aid. The world trees are dying. Their angels cry out to us. The trees roots feed the world and the world feeds the trees. Each requires other as we require you. But the roots are rotting. The nuclear heart dims. There is hope. Cross the great trench to Xanador. Find the veps. The tre's genes may live in them. Find the ve. Renew this tree. Beware. Crypttors slumber beneath the trees. They fear you. We leave them to your mercy."
4.  **Axum Language Interpretation**: The **Axum language** refers to the Titan-class life form on the horizon as a "world tree." The message further indicates the presence of an alien hibernation crypt below this world tree, designated as "Sanitadu," with its status currently unknown.
