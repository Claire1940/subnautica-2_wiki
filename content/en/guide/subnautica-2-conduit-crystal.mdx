<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Acquisition Process">

Acquiring **Conduit Crystal** is a multi-step process that demands preparation and careful execution. Follow these steps to maximize your chances of success and minimize risks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build a Scanner Room">
    Construct a **Scanner Room** at a strategic location close to the estimated crystal hotspots. Equip it with all available range and speed upgrade modules to enhance its detection capabilities. This is your primary tool for pinpointing exact crystal deposits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare Your Vehicle">
    You'll need a vehicle capable of deep dives, such as a **Cyclops** or **Prawn Suit**, equipped with the necessary depth modules. Ensure it has sufficient power, repair tools, and storage space. Consider adding a perimeter defense system for the Cyclops or a grappling arm for the Prawn Suit for maneuverability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scan for Conduit Crystal">
    Once your Scanner Room is operational, set it to scan for "Conduit Crystal." The scanner will highlight crystal nodes within its range, providing a waypoint. Prioritize nodes that are clustered together for efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate to the Location">
    Pilot your deep-diving vehicle towards the detected waypoint. Be mindful of environmental hazards, including aggressive fauna and extreme depths. Use your vehicle's sonar and lights to navigate safely through dark and complex biomes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Harvest the Crystals">
    Upon reaching the crystal deposit, exit your vehicle (if safe) or use your Prawn Suit's drill arm to harvest the **Conduit Crystal**. Each large deposit typically yields multiple crystals. Be aware that hostile creatures may be attracted to your presence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
Consider bringing a mobile vehicle bay to set up a small temporary base near the crystal deposits. This allows you to recharge power cells, store harvested crystals, and repair your vehicle without making long trips back to your main base.
</Callout>
