### ESRB Rating Summary for Subnautica 2

The official ESRB rating for Subnautica 2 highlights two primary content descriptors: **Fantasy Violence** and **Language**. These descriptors explain the elements within the game that contribute to its age recommendation.

**Fantasy Violence**
This descriptor refers to instances where players encounter large alien creatures in the underwater environment. These creatures are depicted biting, striking, and, in some cases, eating the player's character. While the violence is fantasy-based and not graphic, the predatory nature of some leviathans can be intense. Players can use tools and submersibles to defend themselves, but survival often involves evasion rather than direct combat against the largest threats.

**Language**
The game includes mild language. Specifically, the ESRB noted the appearance of the words “a*s” and “p*ssed” within the game's dialogue or text. This level of language is generally considered appropriate for a 10+ audience but is a factor for parental consideration.

<Callout type="warning">
Despite the E10+ rating, the presence of predatory creatures and mild language means parents should consider their child's individual sensitivity to such content.
</Callout>
