<Section icon={Zap} title="Navigating the Deep Caves">

The path to the **subnautica 2 keycard** authority is paved with bioluminescent flora and dangerous crevices. Once you leave your vehicle, you must move quickly. The Blackbox is attached to a distinct purple tendril-like structure at the bottom of the cavern.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Park and Prep">
    Bring your Tadpole vehicle to its maximum safe depth (around 200-250m depending on upgrades). Ensure your oxygen tank is full before exiting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Cave Mouth">
    Look for a narrow opening 210m Southwest of the pens. The walls are often lined with glowing moss that can help guide your path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Descend to the Tendrils">
    Swim down until you see large, purple tendril structures. The Zip Blackbox emits a faint blue beacon light that is visible from a short distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve the Authority">
    Interact with the Blackbox. Your PDA will confirm that "Authority Level: Zip" has been added to your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you run low on air, look for "Oxygen Plants" scattered along the cave floor. These are life-savers during the long swim back to your vehicle. Once you have the data, the **subnautica 2 keycard** is effectively yours.

| Cave Hazard | Danger Level | Survival Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Oxygen Depletion** | Extreme | Use Oxygen Plants / Carry a spare tank |
| **Navigation Loss** | High | Use Pathfinders or follow the glowing moss |
| **Crush Depth** | Medium | Monitor vehicle health alerts constantly |

<Callout type="success" title="Authority Confirmed">
Once you interact with the Blackbox, the "Authority" is permanently tied to your character. You do not need to worry about losing it if you die on the way back, though retrieving your items is still recommended!
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Unlocking the Pens and Lore">

With the **subnautica 2 keycard** authority secured, return to the Tadpole Pens. The journey back is much easier now that you have a clear goal. Enter the facility, turn left at the main door, and interact with the console that previously denied you access.

Upon successful authorization, the door will slide open. Follow these final steps to complete the mission:

- **Climb the Ladder**: Once through the door, look for a ladder leading upward.
- **Enter the Dry Zone**: The upper level of the Tadpole Pens is a pressurized dry area with its own oxygen supply.
- **Access the Computer**: Interact with the main terminal to download research data and lore entries.

This terminal provides critical context regarding the "Tadpole" creatures and the purpose of the facility. Completing this quest often triggers the next major story beat in your 2026 playthrough.

| Reward Category | Item/Data | Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Resources** | Interior Loot | Variable (Check crates for fragments) |
