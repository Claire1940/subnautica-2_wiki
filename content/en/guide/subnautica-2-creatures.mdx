<Section icon={Shield} title="Essential Survival Strategies Against Hostile Fauna">
Surviving the alien ocean of Subnautica 2 requires adapting proven tactics from previous Subnautica titles, alongside new tools like Biomods. Early identification and evasion are key to avoiding fatal encounters.

<Callout type="success" title="Survival Checklist">
Implement these strategies to maximize your chances of survival against aggressive **Subnautica 2 creatures**:
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scan from a Safe Distance">
    Always use your **Scanner tool** from several meters away. Each successful scan populates your PDA databank with crucial information on biology, behavior, and biome. You don't need to kill creatures to learn about them; knowledge is your best defense.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Listen for Leviathan Audio Cues">
    Every Leviathan has a distinct audio cue that precedes its appearance. Pay close attention to these sounds. By the time you see their silhouette, you're likely already too close. Adjust your audio settings to enhance these vital warnings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Carry a Survival Knife">
    While not ideal for direct combat with large predators, a **Survival Knife** can be a last resort against creatures like the Marrowbreach. However, direct engagement is brutal; prioritize detours and evasion whenever possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize the Seaglide and Biomods">
    Outrunning a creature is almost always safer than fighting it. The **Seaglide** significantly reduces travel time, allowing you to break chases effectively. Equip Biomods like **Dash** for enhanced speed and agility, especially when navigating on foot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Mark Dangerous Territories with Beacons">
    Place **Beacons** at the edges of Leviathan roaming territories. These markers are visible from a distance and through terrain, providing your crew with clear indicators of danger zones. This proactive approach can prevent accidental incursions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
