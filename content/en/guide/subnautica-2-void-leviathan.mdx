<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies Against Void Leviathans">
Surviving an encounter with a Shiver Leviathan pack in the Subnautica 2 Void requires quick thinking, proper equipment, and a clear escape plan. Direct combat is generally not recommended given their numbers and aggression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Vehicle">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Warning Signs">
    Pay attention to environmental cues. The Void is often darker and more barren than other biomes. If you see multiple large creature signatures on your sonar, prepare for an encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Situational Awareness">
    Constantly monitor your surroundings. Shiver Leviathans will approach from multiple directions. Use your sonar to track their positions and predict their movements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prioritize Evasion">
    Once engaged, focus all efforts on escaping the Void. Boosters, speed upgrades, and evasive maneuvers are critical. Do not attempt to fight them head-on.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Use Decoys (If Available)">
    If you have any defensive modules that deploy decoys, use them to distract the Shiver Leviathans, buying precious time for your escape.
  </Step>
</Steps>
