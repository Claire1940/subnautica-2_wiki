<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Trial of Faith: Step-by-Step">

The "Trial of Faith" is the final sequence of the **subnautica 2 ending**. It is not a traditional boss fight, but rather a test of the player's understanding of the ecosystem and their willingness to "renew the tree." This involves a series of precise interactions with the Ancient Heart while managing oxygen and biological interference.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Decipher the Auxin Message">
    Collect three linguistic fragments from the surrounding Titan-class life forms to unlock the "Trial of Faith" protocol in your PDA.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Descent to the Ancient Heart">
    Navigate the root system of the World Tree. You must follow the "thrumming" sound, which acts as an acoustic guide through the darkness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Renewal Ritual">
    Insert the Heart Fragments into the central thrumming core. This requires precise timing as the roots move in a rhythmic, lung-like fashion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Great Change">
    Witness the "Great Change Decided" cinematic. The World Tree enters a state of slumber, and the "fear leaves them to your mercy."
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Ending Trigger">

</Callout>

The ending suggests that these trees "hold the timber" of the world itself.
