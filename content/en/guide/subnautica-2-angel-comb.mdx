<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Angel Combs in Subnautica 2">
Angel Combs, scientifically known as *Cecidia gamikos*, are massive Bloom organisms that parasitize Titan Rockbores in the alien ocean of Subnautica 2. These unique structures are crucial for your progression, as they contain gene sequences that grant powerful Adaptations, essential for surviving the planet's diverse and often hostile environments. When infected, an Angel Comb transforms into a Bloom Core, which can only be accessed by neutralizing its connected Bloom Nodes.

<Section icon={Target} title="Clearing the First Angel Comb (Heat Tolerance)">
The first **Angel Comb** is a multi-stage challenge located approximately **200 meters northwest of your Lifepod, bearing about 300 degrees on the compass**, within a cave system. Successfully clearing this will grant you the **Heat Tolerance Adaptation**, allowing you to explore the dangerous lava vent areas without sustaining damage. This Adaptation is critical for continuing the story, investigating the Tadpole Pens, and gathering valuable resources like gold.

<Callout type="warning" title="Preparation is Key">
Before attempting to clear the first Angel Comb, ensure you have the necessary tools and oxygen capacity. Failing to prepare can lead to dangerous situations, especially with aggressive fauna present.
</Callout>

### Essential Gear for the First Angel Comb

| Item                  | Purpose                                   | Acquisition                                             |
| :-------------------- | :---------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Sonic Resonator**   | Destroy viral flowers                     | Craftable (schematic usually found early game)          |
| **Standard Air Tank** | Increase O2 capacity                      | Craftable at Fabricator                                 |
| **Air Bladder**       | Rapid ascent for emergency O2             | Craftable at Fabricator                                 |
| **Survival Multitool** | Clear Bloom Sap for oxygen                | Starting gear                                           |
| **Distraction Flare** | Distract large predatory fish             | Craftable (useful for central chamber)                  |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Angel Comb">
    Head **200 meters northwest of your Lifepod** (compass bearing 300 degrees) to find the cave system containing the first Angel Comb.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Destroy Viral Flowers on Tendrils">
    Follow each of the large, main tendrils branching out from the central comb chamber. Each tendril leads to a smaller chamber filled with murky water and **viral flower growths**. Use your **Sonic Resonator** to destroy these flowers. Be wary of larvae-like fish that will attack. Charge your Sonic Resonator and use the Dash ability to quickly eliminate them. There are typically two such chambers to clear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Oxygen Levels">
    If you find your oxygen levels running low while exploring the tendril chambers, look for funnels blocked by **Bloom Sap**. Use your **Survival Multitool** to hack away the sap and access an oxygen-rich area for a quick refill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clear the Central Chamber">
    Once all the viral flowers in the tendril chambers are destroyed, return to the central chamber. This area is often patrolled by larger, more aggressive predatory fish. **Toss out a distraction flare** to occupy the predator, then swiftly use your **Sonic Resonator** to blast the remaining viral flowers located directly around the Angel Comb.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Claim Heat Tolerance Adaptation">
    After all viral flowers are destroyed, the Angel Comb will open. Interact with it to claim your **Heat Tolerance Adaptation**. You are now ready to explore the lava zones!
  </Step>
</Steps>
</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Conquering the Alien Ruins Angel Comb (Axum Vision)">
Later in your Subnautica 2 journey, you'll encounter a second **Angel Comb** within the **Alien Ruins** area. This one presents a unique challenge: its viral blooms close up when you approach, making them impervious to standard Sonic Resonator blasts. To overcome this, specific upgrades are required. Clearing this Angel Comb unlocks the **Axum Vision Adaptation**, which is essential for interacting with alien devices and beginning repairs on the Alien Power Plant located directly above.

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Strategy">
The Alien Ruins Angel Comb requires a tactical shift. The Feedback Resonator upgrade is crucial as it allows you to destroy the elusive viral blooms from a safe distance before they can close.
</Callout>

### Preparations for the Alien Ruins Angel Comb

| Requirement                     | Description                                                               | Benefit                                                                   |
| :------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Feedback Resonator Upgrade**  | Fires projectiles, allowing destruction of viral blooms from a distance.  | Overcomes the bloom-closing mechanism of the Alien Ruins Angel Comb.      |
| **Tadpole Depth Module**        | Increases the Tadpole submersible's depth capacity to over 250 meters.   | Necessary to access deeper sections of the Alien Ruins where blooms are. |

### Locating and Clearing the Alien Ruins Angel Comb Sections
There are three distinct sections of viral blooms to clear before you can access the main Angel Comb in the Alien Ruins. Follow the large purple tubes branching off from the central comb to find them.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Under the Alien Ruins Research Base">
    Descend into the cavern located directly beneath the plateau where the Alien Ruins Research Base is situated. This is often the easiest of the three sections to find. Destroy the viral flowers here using your **Feedback Resonator**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Branching Side Ravine">
    Head east-southeast from the Alien Ruins Research Base and descend the cliff. You'll spot a large purple tube leading towards the main Angel Comb. Follow this tube, but look for a split where another purple tube branches off to the right, before reaching the central comb. Follow this branch into a small ravine to find and clear the next viral bloom area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Under a Shipwreck">
    This is often the trickiest section. Follow the main purple tube leading to the central Angel Comb, then head northeast along a purple tube that extends through a ravine. Just before you reach the area with the green-glowing pools (where a leviathan patrols), you'll see a purple tube entering a cliff face to the right. Follow this, ascend the cliff, and you'll find a crashed ship with Bloom Biofilm blocking its entrance. Blast the biofilm open, navigate through the ship, and you'll discover the final viral bloom in a cave underneath. Remember to utilize oxygen tunic plants for O2 refills if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clear the Main Angel Comb">
    Once all three branching sections are clear, return to the main Angel Comb area in the Alien Ruins. Destroy the remaining viral blooms directly around the central comb. Upon their destruction, the Angel Comb will open, allowing you to claim the **Axum Vision Adaptation**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
