<Section icon={Target} title="Fragment Location Walkthrough">

Finding the **subnautica 2 feedback resonator** fragments requires precise navigation. Starting from the central starting area, your first landmark is the Alien Ruins, located approximately 1,200 meters directly East.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach the Alien Ruins">
    Travel East from the starting point for 1,200m. You will see a massive tower structure—this is your primary navigation hub. The ruins are part of the main quest, so you may already have a beacon here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Green Zone">
    From the Alien Ruins tower, set your bearing between 60 and 75 (Northeast to East). Travel approximately 850m further. You will notice the water turning a murky green with floating spores.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate Fragment One">
    Descend into the trench. You will find large, alien-like structures. Fragment one is located on a metal ledge near the base of these structures. Be wary of the "Dart Spitters" that patrol this area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate Fragment Two">
    From the first fragment, head Southeast (Bearing 165) for approximately 131m. This fragment is usually tucked behind a rock face or near a cluster of Celestine crystals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Blueprint Unlocked">
Once both fragments are scanned, the Feedback Resonator blueprint will appear in your Modification Station under the "Prototype Tools" tab.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Crafting and Assembly">

With all materials in your inventory, return to your base or a mobile modification unit. The crafting process for the **subnautica 2 feedback resonator** is a multi-step procedure involving two different stations.

1.  **Configurator Processor**: Use this station to combine your raw metals into **Strongtanium**. This is a high-density alloy that serves as the casing for the resonator's feedback loop.
2.  **Fabricator**: Craft two pieces of **Enameled Glass** using the Creature Enamel found near the ruins and standard Glass.
3.  **Modification Station**: Open the "Prototype Tool" menu. Select your existing Sonic Resonator and combine it with the Strongtanium, Enameled Glass, Conduit, and Celestine.
