<Section icon={Shield} title="Avoidance and Survival Strategies">

Surviving an encounter with the **Collector Leviathan** relies heavily on prevention and strategic evasion. Direct combat is rarely a viable option.

### Essential Avoidance Steps

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Maintain Distance">
    Always observe Leviathans from a safe distance. Do not approach them unnecessarily, even for scanning. Your personal safety bubble is your most valuable asset.
    <Checklist
      id="distance-checklist"
      items={[
        "Stay at the edge of known Leviathan patrol zones.",
        "Use long-range scanning tools before entering new areas.",
        "Avoid unnecessary light sources in deep, dark biomes."
      ]}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Listen for Audio Mood Cues">
    The Collector Leviathan often gives audio warnings before initiating an aggressive search or attack. Learn to distinguish these cues from ambient sounds.
    <Callout type="info" title="Audio Cues Breakdown">
    - **Low growls/thrumming**: Indicates awareness, but not immediate aggression.
    - **Loud roars/screeches**: Signifies active hunting or direct threat.
    - **Sudden silence**: Often precedes a charge or ambush.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Avoid Provocation">
    Actions like ramming with a vehicle, flashing bright lights directly at it, or lingering too long in its direct line of sight can provoke an attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Retreat">
    If an encounter is unavoidable, focus on fleeing. Have an escape route planned, utilize terrain for cover, and ensure your vehicle (like the Tadpole submersible) has enough power and upgrades for a quick getaway.
    <Callout type="tip" title="Vehicle Escape">
    When escaping in a vehicle, try to move erratically to make yourself a harder target. Utilize boosters if available, and head towards shallower, safer waters or dense terrain.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>
