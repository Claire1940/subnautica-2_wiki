<Section icon={Shield} title="Progression Milestones: Reaching the Current Endgame">

To see everything in the current build, you must follow a specific progression path. The "end" of the current Early Access story involves unlocking the **Angel Combs** and accessing the power plant's alien controls. This requires a series of upgrades that are often non-obvious to new players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Tadpole & Basic Tools">
    Construct your first vehicle and the Bio-scanner. Focus on scanning everything in the starting shallows to unlock the Wave Maker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Depth Module MK1">
    Located in the **Needler Nest**. You must dive below 250m (abandoning your vehicle temporarily) to find the blueprint next to a colonist's light.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conduit Crystal Farming">
    Search the deepest alien roots (250m+) for tree-shaped crystal formations. These are required for the advanced Bio-scanner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Bio-mod Adaptations">
    Unlock at least 4 of the 6 available character adaptations, including high-temperature resistance, to explore the volcanic vents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The current "bottleneck" for most players is the **Conduit Crystal**. These do not show up on standard scanners easily and are only found near the giant purple roots connected to Angel Combs. Finding these allows you to open the alien controls in the power plant, which is effectively the final narrative step in the May 2026 build.

| Essential Tool | Primary Use | Key Ingredient |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wave Maker** | High-speed underwater travel | Standard Battery |
| **Bio-scanner** | Scanning alien technology | Conduit Crystals |
| **Portable O2 Gen** | Emergency air in deep caves | Lithium / Scanned Parts |
| **Strong Acid** | Advanced crafting components | Necrolyst Cysts |
