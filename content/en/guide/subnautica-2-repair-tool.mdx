<Section icon={Swords} title="Crafting the Repair Tool">
Once you have successfully scanned all the necessary blueprint fragments, the **Subnautica 2 Repair Tool** will become available for crafting at your Fabricator. Crafting this tool requires a few common resources that you should be able to find relatively easily in the early game.

### Required Materials for Repair Tool

| Material | Quantity | Primary Source | Notes |
|:---------|:---------|:---------------|:------|
| **Silicone Rubber** | 1 | Creepvine Seed Cluster | Processed from Creepvine Seed Clusters found in Kelp Forests. |
| **Titanium** | 1 | Metal Salvage, Limestone Outcrops | Abundant in early game areas, often found as Metal Salvage or by breaking Limestone Outcrops. |

<Callout type="success" title="Easy Crafting">
Both Silicone Rubber and Titanium are fundamental early-game resources. You should have no trouble gathering them quickly, making the **Subnautica 2 Repair Tool** accessible even in your initial hours of gameplay.
</Callout>

### Crafting Process

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Materials">
    Collect one Creepvine Seed Cluster from a Kelp Forest biome to craft **Silicone Rubber**. Find Metal Salvage or Limestone Outcrops for **Titanium**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access Fabricator">
    Return to your Lifepod or an established base and interact with your Fabricator.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select and Craft">
    Navigate through the crafting menu to find the **Subnautica 2 Repair Tool** under the "Tools" category. Select it and initiate the crafting process.
  </Step>
</Steps>
