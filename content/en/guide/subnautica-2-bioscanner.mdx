<Section icon={Map} title="Locating the Subnautica 2 Bioscanner">
The Bioscanner is a vital upgrade in Subnautica 2, allowing players to delve deeper into the mysteries of alien structures and enigmatic lifeforms. Unlike the standard scanner, the Bioscanner provides critical insights into Axum technology, which is indispensable for progression. To acquire this advanced tool, players must venture into the treacherous Alien Ruins, specifically targeting the Cicada Wreck (EVA Prep).

This wreck is situated far to the east of your starting Lifepod, past the Tadpole Pens. It's highly recommended to establish a temporary base or at least a waypoint at the Research Outpost within the Alien Ruins before proceeding, as the journey can be extensive. The Cicada Wreck is often found near a large alien turbine, east-northeast of the Alien Ruins region.

**Video Highlights:**
- Travel approximately 1,200 meters from the Lifepod to the Research Outpost in the Alien Ruins.
- Head towards the east-southeastern section, or between the E and SE marks (around 120 degrees).
- The Bioscanner is located inside a wreckage, accessible without specific quest progression.
- Bring a Repair Tool to open doors and facilitate movement within the wreck.

<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating the Cicada Wreck and Retrieving the Bioscanner">
Entering the Cicada Wreck requires careful navigation and the right tools. The wreck can be dark and disorienting, but following a clear path will lead you directly to the Bioscanner blueprint. Always keep your scanner equipped to identify potential resources and other scannable items along the way.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter the Wreckage">
    Locate the yellow door on the left-hand side of the main ship structure. Use your **Repair Tool** to fix the doorway and gain access. The main room often contains several EVA Suits in pods and potentially Improved Fins in a crate, which you can scan.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Key Items">
    Proceed to the next room. Here, you'll find the Iso blackbox and the **Photovoltaic Charger**, a useful upgrade module for your Tadpole. Be sure to scan the **Modification Station** blueprint if you haven't already, as you'll need it later to craft the Bioscanner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Descend to the Bioscanner">
    Find a shaft leading downwards. Descend into this shaft. Keep an eye out for air tanks or bubbling vents that provide oxygen replenishment as you go. Continue downwards, following a bright red light.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Scan the Bioscanner Blueprint">
    At the very bottom of the shaft, you will discover the **Bioscanner** itself. Approach it and use your standard scanner to scan the device. This action will unlock the crafting blueprint for the Bioscanner in your fabricator system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Exit the Wreck">
    You can either retrace your steps back up the shaft or use your **Repair Tool** to fix an alternative door for a quicker exit from the wreckage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Oxygen Management">
The Cicada Wreck can be deep, and oxygen management is crucial. Always keep an eye on your oxygen levels and utilize any available air pockets or bubbling vents to avoid suffocation.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Crafting and Utilizing the Bioscanner">
Once you have the Bioscanner blueprint, the next step is to craft it. This requires specific materials that can be found within the Alien Ruins biome. The Bioscanner is crafted at a Modification Station, which you should have scanned earlier or find within the wreckage itself.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Crafting Requirements">
    To craft the Bioscanner, you will need:
    - **1x Scanner**: Your basic scanner tool.
    - **2x Enameled Glass**: Found in the Alien Ruins, appearing as clumps with multiple protrusions.
    - **3x Conduit Crystal**: Located in the Alien Ruins, resembling kelp-like objects near corrupted Rockbore/Angel Combs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bioscanner Usage">
    The Bioscanner significantly enhances your exploration capabilities:
    - **Alien Technology**: It allows you to scan previously unscannable Axum technology, such as the massive turbines at the Karakorum Power Plant.
    - **Lifeform Analysis**: You can scan various alien lifeforms to unlock new **Biomods** at the Biolab, expanding your personal adaptations.
    - **Enhanced Data**: Provides more detailed information on scanned objects compared to the standard scanner.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
