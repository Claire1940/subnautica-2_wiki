<Section icon={Target} title="How to Get the Hydraulic Fluid Blueprint">

Before you can fix the bridge, you must understand the technology behind it. Unlike many other blueprints that are found in data boxes or scattered wrecks, the **subnautica 2 hydraulic fluid** blueprint is found exactly where it is needed. This design choice ensures that players don't have to wander aimlessly across the ocean to find a random fragment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Maintenance Panel">
    Walk onto the bridge from the Glacial Bay side. On the right-hand side of the bridge structure, you will find a panel labeled "Maintenance Access Point." 
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find the Ruptured Fragment">
    Look at the floor directly in front of the maintenance panel. You will see a yellow, broken container. This is the Ruptured Hydraulic Fluid fragment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scan for the Blueprint">
    Equip your Scanner tool and scan the ruptured container. Once the scan is complete, the blueprint for Hydraulic Fluid will be added to your PDA under the "Advanced Materials" tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Don't Miss It">
Many players accidentally overlook the fragment because it blends into the metal floor of the bridge. If you can't find it, look for the empty slot in the maintenance panel; the fragment is usually lying right beneath it.
</Callout>

| Blueprint Source | Location Detail | Interaction Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ruptured Container** | Bridge Floor, Glacial Bay | Manual Scan |
| **PDA Category** | Resources > Advanced Materials | Automatic Entry |
| **Crafting Station** | Standard Fabricator | Crafting Required |

</Section>

<Section icon={Hammer} title="Crafting and Installation Process">

Once you have secured the four Gel Sacks and the single Creepvine Seed Cluster, it is time to return to your Fabricator. The process of creating **subnautica 2 hydraulic fluid** is instantaneous once the materials are present. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Fabricator">
    Return to your base or your Sea Truck's Fabricator Module. Navigate to the "Resources" tab, then "Advanced Materials."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fabricate the Fluid">
    Select the Hydraulic Fluid icon. One unit will be produced, consuming all five ingredients.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Return to the Bridge">
    Travel back to the Glacial Bay bridge. It is often faster to use a Snowfox or a Seaglide if you parked your main vessel at the dock.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Insert the Fluid">
    Interact with the empty slot in the Maintenance Access Point. The fluid container will slide into place, and the system lights will turn green.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extend the Bridge">
    Walk to the main control console and select "Extend." The bridge will deploy, connecting you to the Arctic Spires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Achievement Unlocked">
Upon extending the bridge for the first time, the "Necessary Repairs" achievement will trigger. This marks a major milestone in the game's progression.
</Callout>

| Action | Result | System Feedback |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Insert Fluid** | Restores System Pressure | Maintenance Panel turns Green |
| **Press Extend** | Mechanical Deployment | Bridge moves into position |
| **Cross Bridge** | Biome Transition | Loading Arctic Spires region |

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Surviving the Arctic Spires">

Repairing the bridge with the **subnautica 2 hydraulic fluid** is only the beginning. The region you have just unlocked, the Arctic Spires, is one of the most hostile environments on Planet 4546B. Unlike the underwater biomes, the threats here are terrestrial and weather-based.

The primary threat in this area is the Ice Worm, a massive leviathan-class creature that tunnels through the ice. It is sensitive to vibrations, meaning your Snowfox will attract it quickly. Using the **hydraulic fluid** to open the bridge allows you to bring vehicles over, but you must use them wisely to avoid becoming a snack for the local predators.

<Callout type="warning" title="Ice Worm Strategy">
If you hear a deep rumbling beneath your feet, stop moving immediately. The Ice Worm tracks movement. Deploying a Thumper will create a decoy vibration, allowing you to escape while the creature attacks the device.
</Callout>
