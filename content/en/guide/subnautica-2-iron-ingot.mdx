<Section icon={Hammer} title="Crafting Steps for Iron Ingots">

Once your lockers are full of raw ore or metal salvage, it is time to head to the Fabricator. The process of creating a **subnautica 2 iron ingot** is straightforward, but it requires a specific ratio of materials. In the 2026 version of the game, the efficiency of your fabricator can be upgraded to reduce power consumption during this intensive smelting process.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Raw Materials">
    Collect 5 units of Raw Iron Ore or 2 pieces of Large Metal Salvage. Ensure your base has at least 50 units of stored power before starting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Fabricator">
    Navigate to the 'Resources' tab, then select the 'Basic Materials' sub-menu. The Iron Ingot icon should be visible if you have the materials in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Processing">
    Click the icon to begin the 3-second fabrication process. The Fabricator will consume the raw materials and output one refined ingot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Storage and Cleanup">
    Place the finished ingot into a dedicated 'Heavy Metals' locker. Converting raw ore to ingots reduces your storage footprint by 80%.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Bonus">
If you use an Industrial Fabricator found in the later biomes, you can process "Batch Ingots," which creates 3 ingots at once for a slightly reduced energy cost.
</Callout>

| **Raw Iron Ore** | 5 Units | 1 Iron Ingot | 5 Energy |
| **Metal Salvage** | 2 Units | 1 Iron Ingot | 7 Energy |
