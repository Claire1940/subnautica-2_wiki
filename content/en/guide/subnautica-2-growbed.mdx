<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Acquisition Guide">

Unlocking the **Subnautica 2 growbed** requires a systematic approach. Unlike some blueprints that only require a single scan, the growbed is divided into two fragments. Both are located within the same vicinity, so you won't need to travel across different biomes to complete the set.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach the Alien Area">
    Progress through the main story until you are directed to the power plant zone. Look for the massive Rosetta Stone tower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate North-Northeast">
    From the base of the Rosetta Stone, set your compass to North-Northeast. Swim toward the smaller structures situated on the outskirts of the main alien complex.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scan the Roof Fragment">
    Locate the small rectangular outpost. Swim to the roof. You will find the first growbed fragment sitting exposed on the top surface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Scan the Ground Fragment">
    From the roof, look to your left and descend to the ground level. The second fragment is typically found sitting in the dirt or on a small platform adjacent to the building.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Oxygen Management">
The Alien Area is deep. Ensure you have at least a High Capacity O2 Tank or a nearby vehicle before attempting to scan both fragments, as the vertical movement between the roof and ground can consume significant air.
</Callout>

While the **Subnautica 2 growbed** found in the Alien Area usually unlocks the standard versions, advanced variants may require additional materials.

<Section icon={Zap} title="Optimizing Your Underwater Farm">

Once you have the **Subnautica 2 growbed** installed, you need to optimize its output. Farming is not just about planting seeds; it is about managing growth cycles and ensuring you have a backup supply of seeds for replanting.

**Key Farming Strategies:**
- **Seed Harvesting**: Use your survival knife on plants to gather seeds. Some plants, like melons, must be hit with a knife to yield seeds, while others can be picked by hand.
- **Lighting and Growth**: Ensure your base has consistent power. While plants don't strictly require "light" mechanics like real-world photosynthesis, power outages can occasionally stall the growth interface in certain base modules.
- **Space Management**: Don't over-plant one species. Balance your growbeds between high-calorie food and high-hydration fruits.

| Plant Name | Growth Speed | Primary Benefit | Harvest Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Marblemelon** | Fast | High Food/Water | Knife (for seeds) |
| **Lantern Fruit** | Medium | Decent Food/Light | Hand Pick |
| **Bulbo Tree** | Slow | Excellent Water | Knife |
| **Creepvine** | Medium | Silicone/Fiber | Hand (Cluster)/Knife (Seed) |

<Callout type="info" title="Advanced Tip">
You can place Exterior Growbeds at various depths. Some deep-sea flora found later in the game will only thrive in growbeds placed below 500 meters. Always check the plant's native biome depth before transplanting.
</Callout>

</Section>
