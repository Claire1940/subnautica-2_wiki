<Section icon={Shield} title="Crafting the Survival Multi-Tool for Harvesting">
Before you can harvest any **Acidic Rain Pouches**, you'll need the right equipment: the **Survival Multi-Tool**. This versatile tool is indispensable for various tasks in Subnautica 2, including resource gathering, repairing, and even some basic combat. <Callout type="warning" title="Tool Requirement">

Here's a simple guide to crafting your Survival Multi-Tool:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Fabricator">
    Head to your **Fabricator**, usually located within your lifepod or a base. The Fabricator is your primary crafting station for tools, equipment, and components.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Personal Tools">
    Within the Fabricator interface, select the **"Personal Tools"** category. This section contains all handheld equipment you can craft.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Materials">
    The Survival Multi-Tool requires **three (3) units of Titanium**. Titanium is a very common resource, often found by breaking down metal salvage or collecting small titanium deposits scattered across the seafloor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Craft the Multi-Tool">
    Once you have the required Titanium, select the **Survival Multi-Tool** from the list and initiate crafting. It will be added to your inventory upon completion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
