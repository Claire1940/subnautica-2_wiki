## Survival Tips for Portable Players

If you are planning to play Subnautica 2 via cloud streaming on your phone, here are a few tips to ensure you don't lose your progress to a connection drop:
1.  **Save Often:** Cloud sessions can occasionally timeout. Make it a habit to save your game every time you return to your base.
2.  **Adjust the UI:** High-resolution text can be hard to read on a small phone screen. Check the in-game settings for UI scaling options to make your PDA easier to navigate.
3.  **Use a Controller:** Touch controls are notoriously difficult for first-person survival games. A clip-on controller will significantly improve your ability to escape predators.
4.  **Monitor Your Data:** Streaming games consumes a massive amount of data. If you aren't on an unlimited plan, stick to Wi-Fi to avoid surprise charges on your mobile bill.
