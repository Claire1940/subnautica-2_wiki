Subnautica 2 takes place on a completely different planet from the previous titles. This means you won't be seeing the familiar Reefbacks or Stalkers of Planet 4546B. Instead, you will encounter a brand-new ecosystem filled with bioluminescent life and terrifying predators.

The "Collector Leviathan" is one of the first major threats players will encounter. This massive, reactive apex predator responds to light, sound, and the presence of vehicles. Surviving an encounter with a Collector requires patience and observation. Experienced players suggest moving slowly and turning off lights when you hear its distinct audio cues.

The map in Subnautica 2 is designed with "depth progression" in mind. You will start in safer, shallow routes where resources are plentiful and the water is clear. As you craft better oxygen tanks and submersibles, you will push into darker zones. The introduction of ocean currents adds a new layer of challenge; these currents can pull you into unexpected areas, forcing you to plan your return routes carefully to avoid running out of air.
