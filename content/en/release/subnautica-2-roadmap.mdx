<Section icon={Target} title="Survival Strategies and Resource Management">

Long-term survival in Subnautica 2 hinges on effective resource management, especially concerning food and water. The game encourages players to establish sustainable systems rather than constantly hunting for basic necessities. The roadmap for 2026 will likely introduce more ways to automate and optimize these processes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Fiber Farms">
    As soon as you gain the ability to create farms, prioritize setting up large plots dedicated to growing fibrous pulp. This resource is abundant and grows quickly, forming the backbone of your water supply.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft Water in Processor">
    Utilize the Processor to create water. The alternate recipe requires only three fibrous pulp and takes 30 seconds per unit, providing an infinitely renewable source of hydration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Food Production">
    Focus on cultivating the ingredients for Tamiyaki (Half Moon, Harvest Moon, Blue Moon). These moons are easily found and farmed, allowing you to craft large quantities of healing food.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Inventory Space">
    Fill chests with crafted food and water. A portable chest attached to your Tadpole early on provides crucial extra storage without sacrificing speed, allowing for longer excursions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Infinite Water Supply">
Growing fibrous pulp allows for an infinite supply of water through the Processor. This significantly reduces the need to constantly search for water slugs, freeing up valuable time for exploration and base expansion.
</Callout>

</Section>
