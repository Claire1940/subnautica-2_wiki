One of the most anticipated aspects of **subnautica 2 graphics** is the overhaul of the lighting engine. In the dark, crushing depths of the ocean, light management is everything. *   **Improved Particle Effects:** Expect more realistic bubbles, sediment clouds, and thermal vents.
*   **Dynamic Shadowing:** Light from your vehicle’s floodlights will now interact more realistically with the environment.

*   **Volumetric Fog:** The underwater haze will feel thicker and more oppressive, adding to the survival tension.
